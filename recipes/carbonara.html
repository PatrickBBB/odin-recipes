Carbonara

Description

Pasta carbonara the traditional way, not the bullshit way where you add cream.

Ingredients

  • Dried pasta.
  • 50-100 grams Guanciale. You can substitute this for bacon if you can't find Guanciale.
  • 100 grams cheese - Peccorino romano - can use Parmesan instead
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 egg
  • Paste water - don't trow it away!

Steps

  1. Cut the Guanciale to small bits and add them to a frying pan. Render out the fat at low heat.
  2. Boil the pasta to 1 minute shy of al dente.
  3. Grate the cheese and add the egg yolks and egg to it
  4. Add the pasta to the frying pan. Add 1/2 a cup of the pasta water as well. We need that starch to emulsify a sauce!
  5. Take the pan of the heat. Wait 1 minute, then add the egg-cheese mixture.
  6. Stir the pasta and sauce around untill you have a sauce you are happy with. If needed add more pasta water if you need it. You can also return it to the heat if you need. To much heat makes scrambled egg, we don't want that!
