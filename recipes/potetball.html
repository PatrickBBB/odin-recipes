Potetball

Description

This Norwegian monstrosity is a sight to be seen. It has almost no taste, its incredibly dense and we Norwegians love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg raw potatoes
  • 200 grams boild potatoes
  • 150 grams barley flour
  • 50 grams oats - cooked
  • 250 grams of bacon

Steps

  • Grate the raw potatoes. You can use a grinder if you want.
  • Mash the boiled potatoes and add them to the raw
  • Add the barley flour and the oats.
  • The mixture should be malleable enough to form into balls. Form them to balls and ad them to water that is just under boiling!
