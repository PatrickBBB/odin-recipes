Humba!

Description

Hailing from the island Mindanao in Philippines, this sweet, salty and savory dish is my favorite dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablesppons oil
  • 1.5 pounds pork belly - cut into 1x2-inch chunks
  • 4 cloves garlic-minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 2 cups water - add more if needed
  • 2 pieces of bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon peppercorn
  • 1/4 cup dried banana blossoms
  • 1/4 cup fermented black beans

Steps

  1. Braise the pork belly to golden brown - remove excess oil
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and stir
  3. Cook for 40-50 minutes or longer
  4. Serve with rice!
